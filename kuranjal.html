Kuranjal Trek Overview

Your adventure starts here

  • Starting Point: Balgal or Mullodi Village (via Samse)
  • Elevation: ~1,900 m (6,233 ft)
  • Trek Distance: ~12 km round trip
  • Duration: 5–6 hours total
  • Difficulty: Moderate; suitable for nature lovers

Kudremukh Trek Overview

A challenging adventure

  • Starting Point: Mullodi base camp (via Balgal)
  • Elevation: ~1,894 m (6,214 ft)
  • Trek Distance: ~20–22 km round trip
  • Duration: 7–9 hours total
  • Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging

Highlights & Wildlife

Nature's finest moments

  • Shola forests, open grasslands, and crystal-clear streams
  • Panoramic views of Western Ghats and Kudremukh Peak
  • Hidden waterfalls during monsoon season
  • Wildlife: Deer, lion-tailed macaques, hornbills, occasional leopards

Permits & Rules

Essential information

  • Forest Entry Permit: ₹600–800 per person
  • Guide Mandatory: 1 guide per group (~₹1,000)
  • Trek Timings: Entry from 6 AM; finish by 6 PM
  • 🚫 No overnight camping allowed
  • 🚫 Plastic items, liquor, and smoking prohibited

How to Reach

Getting to the trailhead

  • By Road: Bangalore → Kalasa → Balgal/Mullodi (~330 km)
  • By Train: Nearest station: Mangalore (~110 km)
  • By Bus: From Bangalore/Mangalore to Kalasa or Kottigehara
  • Jeep: From Balgal to Mullodi (~₹500–700 for 6–7 people)

Best Time to Trek

Plan your visit

  • Primary Season (Oct–Feb): Clear skies, lush trails
  • Monsoon (Jun–Sep): Green landscapes, slippery trails, leeches
  • Summer (Mar–May): Hot and dry, less ideal for Kudremukh

Tips & Preparation

Get ready for the trek

  • Start Early: Begin by 6–7 AM for optimal conditions
  • Footwear: Trekking shoes with ankle support, trekking poles
  • Hydration: Carry 2+ liters of water
  • Leech Protection: Leech socks or salt for monsoon
  • Essentials: Raincoat, ID proof, snacks, first-aid kit, power bank
