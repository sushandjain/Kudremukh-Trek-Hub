Trek Overview

Your adventure starts here

  • Starting Point: Mullodi village, ~10 km from Kalasa
  • Elevation: ~1,894 m (6,214 ft)
  • Trek Distance: ~9 km one-way (18 km round trip)
  • Duration: 5–6 hours total
  • Difficulty: Moderate; suitable for beginners with decent fitness

Highlights & Wildlife

Nature's finest moments

  • Lush green valleys and misty grasslands
  • Waterfalls and cloud-covered peaks
  • Rich biodiversity: Malabar civet, langurs, and diverse birdlife
  • Panoramic views from the horse-face shaped summit

Permits & Rules

Essential information

  • Forest Entry Permit: ₹600 per person
  • Guide Mandatory: ~₹500–800 per group
  • Trek Timings: 6 AM to 6 PM only
  • 🚫 No overnight camping allowed
  • 🚫 Plastic items strictly prohibited

How to Reach

Getting to the trailhead

  • By Road: Bangalore → Hassan → Kalasa → Mullodi (~330 km)
  • From Mullodi: Jeep ride to trailhead (~₹600–800, shared)
  • Homestays: Available in Kalasa/Mullodi (~₹800–1,500)

About Henjodi Stores

Henjodi Stores is a local business in Malenadu offering complete support for trekkers visiting peaks like Kudremukh, Netravati, and Kuranjal. We assist with trekking ticket bookings, arranging certified guides, jeep rides, food, and homestays.
